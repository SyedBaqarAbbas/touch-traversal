# Prototype Review

## What held up

The fictional review group could follow highlighted relationships when the selected thought stayed
bright and the surrounding graph became quieter. They understood the [demo route](/demo) without a
tour once the pointer, selection, and return actions used the same visual language. The strongest
moment was moving from [[Wayfinding Study]] into a related note without losing the prior location.

## What changes next

Selection still felt too eager near dense clusters, and fast topology changes made labels difficult
to reacquire. The next pass will use the measured boundaries in [[Gesture Thresholds]] and the
motion rules in [[Quiet Constellations]]. A successful revision should reduce accidental activation
without making intentional traversal feel delayed or mechanical.
